What I Do

I’ve got the skills you need to grow your business

Multiple Data Sources

Being able to read in information from all kinds of sources is probably one of the most important skills, which is often underestimated and neglected by data scientists. Personally, I have extensive experience with working a wide variety of RDMBS and NO-SQL databases, as well as communicating via REST and GraphQL APIs.

Data cleaning

During the XXI. century's first gold rush companies started gathering massive amounts of information under the code words 'Big Data', however, in most cases the underlying purpose of the collection of specific data was unclear, which only lead to companies sitting on top of useless low-quality, unstructured data. I can help you bring order into your chaos!

Data preparation

Once a certain level of data quality is achieved, one can get into the next phase of data preparation. Depending on the purpose, I'll structure the data in the right format for the respective Machine Learning algorithm including feature engineering.

Quantitative analytics

You were always keen on to introduce new KPIs, but due to Excel's limitations, you were unable to? You would love to visualise your data, but traditional tools fail due to the high number of observations? I can help you generating custom reports with the power of R.

Machine learning

Supervised, unsupervised and reinforcement learning techniques help me improving your decision making process by providing real time predictions for the relevant questions. The output is a state-of-the-art prediction model, that can be used in production systems.

Deployment

My machine learning models can be deployed in the cloud in a scalable fashion, which would guarantee high availability, high throughput predictions in the milliseconds time range. But also keeping in mind traditional corporate approaches, the models can be integrated in your local software systems if specific criteria are met.
